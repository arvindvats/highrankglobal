Our Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At HighRank, we are dedicated to protecting and preserving the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy elucidates the handling of any personal data you provide to us or that we collect from you during your visit to our site. We regularly update this Policy, so we encourage you to review it periodically.

Information We Collect

In the operation and maintenance of our website, we may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Information about your site usage, including details of your visits such as pages viewed, and the resources you access. This comprises traffic data, location data, and other communication data.
  • Information provided voluntarily by you, such as when you register for information or purchase a service.
  • Information you provide when communicating with us through any means.

Use of Your Information

We utilize the collected information to deliver our services and may also use it for the following purposes:

  • Providing requested information about our services.
  • Offering information about other services that may be of interest, with your consent.
  • Notifying you of any changes to our website and services.
  • Enhancing our website and customer service to personalize your browsing experience.
  • Processing transactions.
  • Providing details of similar or additional services if you have previously purchased from us.
  • Administering contests, promotions, surveys, or other site features.

Your information will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or disclosed to any third party without your explicit consent, except for delivering the purchased service(s).

Storing Your Personal Data

While the internet transmission of information is not entirely secure, we implement appropriate safeguards upon receiving your data to ensure secure storage. The sending of information electronically is at your own risk.

Use of Cookies

Cookies are used to gather information about your computer to assist in improving our website. These cookies do not personally identify you but provide statistical data about general internet usage. You can adjust your computer settings to decline cookies. Advertisers on our website may use cookies over which we have no control.

Disclosing Your Information

We will only disclose your personal information under the following circumstances:

  • In the event of selling all or part of our business.
  • When legally required by law.
  • To prevent fraud and reduce fraud risk.

Third Party Links

Links to third-party websites on our platform do not imply endorsement or approval of their privacy policies. Prior to providing any personal data, we recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This online privacy policy pertains solely to information collected through our website and not offline.

Your Consent

By using our website, you express consent to our privacy policy.
